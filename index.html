The studio

A third place, in the truest sense.

Between home and work there should be a room like this one: a long table, good tea, and something to make. We bring artisans and traditions from many cultures under one roof — never fixed to a single one, always rooted in what they share.

Craftsmanship

Everything here is made, taught, and chosen by hand — from the season's wagashi to the stones on the shelf.

Mindfulness

Slow practices that quiet a busy week: whisked tea, dye vats, breath, and unhurried conversation.

Connection

Strangers become tablemates. Traditions meet, borrow, and make something new together.

The shop & café

Good things, chosen slowly.

Open daily alongside the beverage bar — everything in the shop is picked for craft and character.

菓

Wagashi

Traditional Japanese confections, handmade with seasonal ingredients. Come see what the season looks like in bean paste and sugar — then taste it with a bowl of tea.

Made in-house · Seasonal
石

Crystals & Minerals

Beaded jewelry, decorative objects, healing stones, and tarot & oracle tools — collected piece by piece, with room to browse slowly.

Curated · One-of-a-kind
茶

Oshicoffee & Matcha Bar

Specialty coffee, matcha lattes, and tins of premium matcha powder to take home — pulled and whisked at the counter all day.

Café · To-go & take-home

Our matcha is sourced from Caiyunjiang （采云间） in Zhejiang, China — one of the country's top matcha producers and a UNESCO-recognized cultural-heritage tea brand, inscribed in 2022.

Events & venue

Host your own yaji.

雅集 yǎjí — "the elegant gathering": the old word for friends meeting over tea, art, and good conversation. Our cozy, well-designed room is yours for exactly that.

Corporate & team offsites

Team-building that people actually remember — pair the room with a dyeing session, a tea ceremony, or a wagashi class.

Family celebrations

Birthdays, reunions, and milestones in a warm, intimate setting — with the café counter close at hand.

Salons & social gatherings

Art salons, elegant meetups, book clubs, and slow evenings among friends old and new.

Custom experiences

Tell us what your group loves — we'll co-design a one-of-a-kind experience around it.
